About Me

Background

I'm currently pursuing a Master's in Information Systems (MSIS) at Indiana University's Kelley School of Business, building upon my strong foundation from my undergraduate studies.

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Business, majored in Information Systems and Business Analytics, graduating with High Distinction. During my undergraduate years, I served as a peer tutor for K201 Foundations of Business Information Systems and Decision Making, helping fellow students master complex technical concepts.

My academic journey combines technical skills with business strategy to solve complex problems in consulting and technology-focused roles. This blend of quantitative analysis and strategic thinking has prepared me well for the advanced coursework in my MSIS program.

Interests

Generative AI, data-driven decision making, and emerging technologies that transform how businesses operate. Outside class: peer tutoring, research projects, and sports like soccer and basketball.

Goals

I aim to work in technology consulting or product roles, helping organizations adopt AI responsibly to deliver measurable impact. Long term, I want to bridge technical innovation and strategic leadership.
